Ideas to teach research communication

There aren't many people who have a full-time job teaching researchers how to communicate their work, but many librarians and research supervisors find themselves doing it every now and then. It might not be strictly "teaching" but some form of guidance. Whether you teach research communication formally or provide advisory support, the ideas on this page might help.
